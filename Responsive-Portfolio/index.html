About Me


My name is Isaac Frishman and I am an opera singer by trade turned web developer. I was born in New York, grew up in Nebraska, lived in Michigan for 5 years, and am now, a resident of Chicago, IL. I have been very fortunate in my young career to have performed all over the world. You can find more information about that here. You can also contact my manager for any music inquiries here

When the pandemic hit, all of the gigs I had up through 2023 got cancelled which made me re-evaluate things that were most important to me. The time I was given allowed me to figure out some new hobbies, passions, etc. After speaking at length to my "board of directors" and doing some soul searching I found myself attempting to learn to code. As I learned that I was able to use my creativity in other ways by making something new, it became something I couldn't stop. The next thing I knew, I was enrolled in a coding bootcamp which allowed me to become a full stack web developer.
